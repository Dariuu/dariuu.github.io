ABOUT DARIUU (Darius Rusu)



A student at the National University of Art in Bucharest (UNArte) as part of the Textile Art - Textile Design department, Dariuu strides to break the boundaries of his work and push his abilities as an artist to the next level.

Dariuu considers himself to be a generalist, actively trying to learn new things to do artistically. From learning alone how to use Photoshop, to learning how to create digital worlds through the power of 3D modeling, digital art, music, video editing etc. , to finally achieving his dream of being accepted into an arts university and finally being able to follow his dreams, he never gives up.

His favourite way of making art is through storytelling. Dariuu loves creating new worlds to play with, figuring out every detail of that world and documenting it through his artworks. “The Depths of The World”, “Nightskins”, “POST.human” are examples of such worlds.
