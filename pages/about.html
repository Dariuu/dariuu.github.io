ABOUT DARIUU



Dariuu is a musical artist making mainly electronic music.

His first album, Alien Damnation released in April 2021, is an instrumental experimental collection of songs that collectively tells a story of a damned civilization and focuses on the unfortunate story of one of the survivors. This story can be discovered through the names of the songs and the instrumental feelings found within them. After taking a small break from working on his next project, Dariuu releases Dæmonium in June 2021 as a small extension to the Alien Damnation universe.

His second album, Stardust from March 2022 is a surprising change in sound for Dariuu's discography. Going from dark ambient instrumentals to beautiful pianos and strings inspired by hip-hop, lo-fi and other influences. Just as his debut album, this one also tell a story. The story of two people who fall apart at the beginning of the record, but by the end of it we can see that they find each other again.

After taking a break from social media and releasing music, Dariuu comes back to electronic music and debuts his songwriting and vocals on a new single titled It's Pretty Dark.

Although Dariuu said that this would be his last project for a while, a sudden release cycle springs into existence. Bedroom Music, Dariuu's second EP is made up of 6 songs, sonically similar to Stardust, explaining the way his mental health has been at an all time low.
