ABOUT DARIUU

Hello!

My name is Darius also known as Dariuu. I am an independent musical artist. I've always loved making art, any kind of art, painting, designing, writing, musical things and others! I've always wanted to make music since I was very young. I remember always being interested in music, always singing some melody in my head, always pretending that I'm playing an instument. Even without any musical preparation, there was always music in me and I loved it.

And one day, I decided that I was finally going to start making some music of my own, as I had always wanted. I didn't have any real instruments or any kind of experience, I only had my computer and the Internet. And that's how my adventure started.

It was a frustating and hard adventure but because I just loved it so much I continued and I finally created and realeased my first singles 'Let's Go!' and 'Adventure' and a month later released my first album 'Alien Damnation' on April 15th.

After this exhaushing weird alien adventure I took somewhat of a break from electronic experimental music and quite bizzarely started making lo-fi type music. I was in love with the vibes I was creating and I couldn't stop making tracks. I even started experimenting with adding small bits of me talking in there to add some kind of meaning and uniqness to each song. The first track I made was called 'will you come with me?' and this song along with 'just checking up on you' became the focus of my latest album, 'stardust'.

While working on said album, I started imagining a whole new enviroment, a dystopian apocalyptic dark enviroment full of violence, madness and sadness. This whole world began exiting the moment I wrote "The Depths of The World" in the middle of the night without any good reason, like it was a gift from the Universe. So I kept writing and envisioning about this dark world and that became the focus of my upcoming album named after that gifted song. I just released It's Pretty Dark and I am still working on this new world.
